About Me

My name is Erik Alfvin and I live in Saint Paul, MN. I am originally from Wisconsin and unfortunately still a Packer fan. I currently work for the State of Minnesota in the Department of Commerce, Weights & Measures Division. We do precision measurement of the mass of items that are used for research purposes and to ensure that scales are accurate.

Outside of work I like to do nordic skiing and develop software for fun personal projects. In the future, I would like to develop software for a scientific or engineering application full time.
